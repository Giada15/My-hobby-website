Overview

I enjoy hiking and exploring new trails with my dog. I live in Waterloo, so I’ve mainly tried to discover the trails in my area. Hiking allows me to disconnect from my busy schedule and enjoy the present moment. It benefits my mental health—being in nature helps me feel calm and reset when I’m stressed. It also keeps me physically active. Since I don’t enjoy other types of exercise, hiking is a great way for me to stay healthy.

Why do I enjoy hiking? It’s a habit I developed as a child. My father would take my sister and me on trips to the woods, mountains, and lakes in Italy, as he loved nature and animals. Though I stopped hiking as I got older, I returned to it because of my dog. As a natural hunter, my dachshund needs regular exercise, so I always look for new places to take her. My hikes are more fun with her by my side. She has a strong instinct to explore, and visiting new places is important for her too.

Another aspect of hiking that I enjoy is encountering wildlife. On our trips, we've seen deer, coyotes, rabbits, and various birds. I’m always excited to spot animals in their natural habitats, and I love the sense of connection to nature it provides.
